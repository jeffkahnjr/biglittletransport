.big little transport.

a smart shuttle dispatcher

Dispatch ride-sharing shuttles in NYC

Identifying efficient pickup/dropoff pairs for shuttling ride-share passengers.

.big little transport. helps ride sharing companies dispatch point to point shuttles to the areas of highest demand. It specifically looks for shuttle routes that have tight clustering of both pickup and dropoff location. This allows the dispatcher to pool together more riders and save on operating costs.
